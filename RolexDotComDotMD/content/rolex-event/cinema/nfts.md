## National Film and Television School

A vanguard of creative excellence

The National Film and Television School (NFTS) is widely regarded as one of the world’s leading schools for screen arts, with alumni collectively holding more than 60 Oscar nominations and over 100 BAFTA awards.

## Nurturing the cinematic arts

Located in the United Kingdom and founded in 1971, the National Film and Television School (NFTS) has become a powerhouse of creativity and innovation, producing graduates who consistently shape the future of the screen industries. Its alumni include Academy Award, BAFTA, and Emmy winners whose works span genres and platforms, reflecting both the breadth and depth of talent cultivated within the school’s halls. In 2024, Rolex deepened its commitment to the arts through a new partnership with the National Film and Television School.

## Pushing boundaries

Now offering over 30 Masters degrees, diplomas and certificate courses, the institution’s hands-on, industry-focused approach ensures that students graduate not only as imaginative storytellers, but also technically skilled collaborators. Over the decades, the NFTS has consistently embraced innovation, adapting its curriculum to reflect the evolving landscape of global media. From virtual production to immersive storytelling, the school fosters a forward-thinking mindset while honouring the fundamental principles of cinematic craft. Its facilities are among the most advanced in the world, and its close ties to industry leaders provide students with direct pathways to professional success.

In 2018, the NFTS received the BAFTA for Outstanding British Contribution to Cinema at the 71st awards. Rolex supports the NFTS’s mission to equip emerging filmmakers, screenwriters, producers, and designers with the skills and vision to excel on the global stage.
