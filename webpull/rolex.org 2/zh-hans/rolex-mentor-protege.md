- ### [安妮・拉卡顿与阿琳・阿布拉哈米安 - 劳力士创艺指导计划](https://www.rolex.org/zh-Hans/rolex-mentor-protege/current-pairs/architecture/anne-lacaton-and-arine-aprahamian)

- ### [贾樟柯与拉斐尔・曼努埃尔 - 劳力士创艺指导计划](https://www.rolex.org/zh-Hans/rolex-mentor-protege/current-pairs/film/jia-zhang-ke-and-rafael-manuel)

- ### [伯纳德・埃瓦里斯托与阿耶莎・哈鲁娜・阿塔 - 劳力士创艺指导计划](https://www.rolex.org/zh-Hans/rolex-mentor-protege/current-pairs/literature/bernardine-evaristo-and-ayesha-harruna-attah)

- ### [戴安・瑞芙与Song Yi Jeon - 劳力士创艺指导计划](https://www.rolex.org/zh-Hans/rolex-mentor-protege/current-pairs/music/dianne-reeves-and-song-yi-jeon)

- ### [艾尔・安纳祖与布朗温・卡兹 - 劳力士创艺指导计划](https://www.rolex.org/zh-Hans/rolex-mentor-protege/current-pairs/visual-arts/el-anatsui-and-bronwyn-katz)

- ### [跳出舒适区 - 劳力士创艺指导计划](https://www.rolex.org/zh-Hans/rolex-mentor-protege/recent-mentorships/film/spike-lee-and-kyle-bell)

- ### [同行旅程 - 劳力士创艺指导计划](https://www.rolex.org/zh-Hans/rolex-mentor-protege/recent-mentorships/theatre/phyllida-lloyd-and-whitney-white)
